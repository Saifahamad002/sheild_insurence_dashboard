# Shield-Insurance-Analysis
This project is a part of the **virtual Internship at AtliQ Technologies.**

## PROJECT OVERVIEW -

**Shield Insurance** aims to improve its data-driven decision-making by developing an interactive dashboard that provides clear and actionable business insights. To evaluate the potential collaboration, the company has initiated a **pilot project with AtliQ Technologies** to assess our analytical and dashboard development capabilities.

This **pilot project** focuses on transforming insurance data into meaningful visual insights, helping the company analyze key areas such as revenue performance, customer distribution, and age group behavior.

The outcome of this pilot will help **Shield Insurance** determine whether **AtliQ Technologies** can effectively meet their requirements and support a potential long-term partnership.

## Data Sets and Data Model -

## Insights -
1️⃣ **Revenue & Customer Growth Trend**

In **February 2023**, revenue remained relatively stable with a slight increase of 1.1%, while customer growth declined by 0.9%.

In **March 2023**, there was a sudden spike, where revenue increased by 85% and customer growth increased by 82.3%.

However, in **April 2023**, both metrics declined sharply:

Revenue decreased by 41.7%

Customer growth decreased by 41.1%

This suggests that a short-term factor such as a marketing campaign, seasonal demand, or promotional offer may have driven the March spike, but it was not sustained in the following month.

2️⃣ **Sales Mode Performance**

In **February**, the sales channels were relatively stable.
The offline segment slightly declined, while online app sales showed growth.

In **March**, both online and offline sales increased significantly, contributing to the revenue spike.

In **April**, revenue declined across all sales modes, but the offline channel experienced the largest drop, while online channels showed a smaller decline.

3️⃣ **Policy Purchase Trends**

The most purchased policies are:

**POL2005HEL**  
**POL1048HEL**  
**POL9221HEL**

These policies are mostly purchased by customers in the age groups

The least purchased policies are:  
**POL4321HEL  
POL4331HEL  
POL3309HEL**

These policies are mainly purchased by younger age groups   
**18–24

4️⃣ **Revenue by Sales Channel**

The majority of revenue comes from **Offline Agents and Online channels**, indicating these are the primary sales drivers.

The least revenue comes from the Offline Direct channel.

5️⃣ **Customer Distribution by Sales Channel**

Most customers purchase policies through **Offline Agents and Online channels**, while Offline Direct contributes the smallest number of customers.

6️⃣ **Settlement Amount by Age Group**

The highest settlement amounts occur in the **31–40 and 41–50** age groups, indicating higher claim activity or policy value in these segments.

The lowest settlement amount occurs in the **18–24** age group, suggesting lower claim frequency or smaller policy values.

## Recommendations -
1️⃣ **Revenue & Customer Growth Trend**

The company should analyze the drivers behind the March spike **(campaigns, promotions, partnerships, or policy launches)** and replicate successful strategies to maintain consistent growth.

2️⃣ **Sales Mode Performance**

Shield Insurance should strengthen its online channels **(app and website)** as they show more stable performance and scalability.
At the same time, improving training and incentives for offline agents can help maintain strong offline sales.

3️⃣ **Policy Purchase Trends**

**Shield Insurance** should review the pricing, coverage, and marketing strategy for low-performing policies to make them more attractive, particularly for younger customers.

4️⃣ **Revenue by Sales Channel**

The company should invest more in the high-performing channels **(Offline Agents and Online platforms)** while evaluating whether the Offline Direct channel requires improvement or strategic changes.

5️⃣ **Customer Distribution by Sales Channel**

To improve customer acquisition, the company should focus on expanding **digital channels and enhancing customer experience** in **online platforms**.

6️⃣ **Settlement Amount by Age Group**

**Shield Insurance** should implement risk-based pricing strategies and targeted **policy offerings** for high-settlement age groups to maintain profitability while managing risk.

## 🔚 Conclusion

This project is a pilot initiative for **Shield Insurance** to demonstrate how Power BI and data analytics can transform raw insurance data into meaningful insights. The dashboard helps analyze revenue trends, customer behavior, and age group segments to support better data-driven decision-making.
